Collecting and Using Your Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

Our Application does not collect any personally identifiable information. All data generated by the game is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Usage Data

The Application collects a limited amount of non-personally identifiable Usage Data to provide core game features. This data is stored exclusively on your device. The data we collect includes:

  • Game Progress: We save your progress in the puzzles, including solved status, time taken to solve a puzzle, and your score.
  • Game Statistics: We store your total points and winning streaks for different puzzle types.

This data is stored in your device's local storage (SharedPreferences) and is necessary for the Application to function as intended.

Use of Your Data

The Application uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the core features of our Service, such as saving your progress and displaying your scores and statistics.
  • To allow you to resume playing daily puzzles that are in progress.

Data Storage and Security

All Usage Data and game progress information is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. The security of your data is your own responsibility as it is stored on your own device.
