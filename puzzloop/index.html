About PuzzLoop

PuzzLoop brings together several bite‑sized puzzle games in one jungle‑themed package. Each puzzle exercises a different part of your brain – memory, logic, attention and spatial thinking – while keeping the experience relaxing and visually cohesive.

Core idea

One app, many mini‑puzzles

  • Daily puzzle mode with a new challenge every day.
  • Practice mode to replay and improve individual puzzles.
  • Global points system and streaks to keep you motivated.
  • Short sessions – most puzzles can be finished in under a few minutes.
Puzzles included

Memory, logic and spatial puzzles

  • Memory puzzle: remember the position of tiles and reveal them in order.
  • Equation puzzle: complete simple equations by choosing the correct numbers.
  • Maze puzzle: find a path through a small maze under light time pressure.
  • Bottle puzzle: sort colorful liquid into bottles so each bottle has just one color.
Progress

Points, streaks and daily challenges

  • Earn points based on your performance in each puzzle.
  • Daily puzzles track your streak – see how many days in a row you can solve them.
  • Global point total shows your long‑term progress.
  • Practice any puzzle freely without affecting your daily streak.

How to play

PuzzLoop is designed so you can jump in quickly. Each mini‑game has its own rules, but the overall structure – points, daily mode and streaks – works the same way.

Daily puzzle mode

  • Tap the menu button in the game and choose Daily puzzle.
  • Each day you get one pre‑generated puzzle with a fixed layout.
  • You have a limited number of hints depending on the game type.
  • Solving the daily puzzle increases your daily streak for that puzzle type.
  • You can view solved daily puzzles again, but they are no longer interactive.

Practice mode

If you just want to practice a specific puzzle, you can pick it directly from the menu:

  • Memory puzzle – flip tiles in the correct order.
  • Equation puzzle – choose numbers to complete the equation.
  • Maze puzzle – drag from start to goal.
  • Bottle puzzle – tap to pour between bottles and sort the colors.

Practice mode does not affect your daily streak, but you still earn points.

Points & streaks

Each puzzle awards points based on time, accuracy or both. The top bar in the game shows:

  • Points: your total accumulated score across all puzzles.
  • Streak: the highest daily streak among all puzzle types.

Daily streaks are tracked separately per puzzle (memory, equation, maze, bottle), so you can specialise or try to keep them all alive.

Hints & audio

Some puzzles support hints if you get stuck. You can also toggle sound effects on or off from the main game screen.

  • Daily mode gives fewer hints than practice mode.
  • Hints are tailored for each puzzle type (e.g. reveal a tile in memory puzzle).
  • Global sound button lets you quickly mute or enable audio feedback.

Support & contact

If you run into issues, have questions or want to suggest new puzzles, feel free to contact the developer.

Is the game free?

Yes, PuzzLoop can be downloaded for free from the Google Play Store. Depending on the version, there may be ads or optional in‑app purchases.

Do I need an internet connection?

You can play PuzzLoop without a constant internet connection. You will need internet to download the game, receive updates and possibly to load ads.

Which devices are supported?

PuzzLoop is designed for Android phones and tablets running Android 8.0 or newer. On the Play Store, you can see compatibility information for your own devices when you are logged in.
