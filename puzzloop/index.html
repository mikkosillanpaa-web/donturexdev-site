PuzzLoop – daily brain puzzles

A colorful jungle-themed collection of quick mini-games designed to sharpen memory, logic and attention.

Puzzles included

Memory Puzzle

Remember tile positions and reveal them in the correct order.

Equation Puzzle

Complete simple equations by selecting the correct numbers.

Maze Puzzle

Find your way through compact jungle mazes.

Bottle Puzzle

Sort colorful liquids so each bottle contains one color only.

SearchPuzzle

Observe the target shapes shown at the top of the screen and find them hidden inside the arena before time runs out. Tests visual attention and pattern recognition.

MindPuzzle

A logic-based code breaking game inspired by Mastermind. Guess the hidden color combination. After each guess, you receive feedback on correct colors and positions. Use deduction to solve it in as few moves as possible.

How to play

Choose Daily mode for a new pre-generated challenge every day or Practice mode to freely train any puzzle type. Points and streaks are tracked per puzzle.

Privacy

No account required. All progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
