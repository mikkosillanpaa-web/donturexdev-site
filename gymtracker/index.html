About GymTracker

GymTracker helps you build a gym program, track your workouts, follow progress and stay consistent. The app combines workout logging, equipment-aware program creation, exercise instructions, rest timers, timed sets, visual history and coaching logic for progression, deloads and exercise variation.

Program

Program creation & equipment preferences

  • Create workout programs based on goal, level, schedule and training split.
  • Use equipment preferences: Prefer, OK, Avoid and Off.
  • Built-in exercise library with machines, cables, dumbbells, barbells, Smith machine, kettlebells, resistance bands and bodyweight movements.
  • Push/pull balance and smarter default rep ranges help create more practical programs.
During workout

Track sets, timers and workout changes

  • Log sets, reps, weights and timed sets directly during the workout.
  • Built-in rest timer with vibration support.
  • Add, remove or replace exercises during a workout — only for this session or update the program.
  • Adapt the current workout for a temporary home or outdoor session.
Coach

Progression, deloads and exercise guidance

  • Coach logic suggests progression, volume changes, deloads and exercise variations.
  • Exercise metadata helps keep suggestions safer and more relevant.
  • Clear localized exercise instructions explain purpose, setup, execution and safety.
  • Bodyweight, resistance band and time-based exercises are handled without unnecessary weight prompts.
History

Visual progress dashboard

  • Weekly overview with completed workouts.
  • Monthly calendar with visual workout markers.
  • Workout summaries, achievements and recent sessions.
  • Tap a calendar day to open the workout details.

How GymTracker works

GymTracker is built around three main flows: creating your program, logging workouts and reviewing progress through history and coach feedback.

1. Create your program

  • Choose your goal, training level, weekly schedule and training split.
  • Set equipment preferences so the program matches your gym setup.
  • Preview the generated program before saving it.
  • You can still edit training days and exercises manually.

2. Log your workouts

  • Start a workout from the Workout tab or a training day detail screen.
  • Tap set chips to mark sets completed; timed exercises can use a built-in timer.
  • Use the rest timer between working sets.
  • Add, remove or replace exercises during the workout when plans change.

3. Review history & coach feedback

  • The History dashboard shows your training week, monthly calendar, summaries and achievements.
  • Tap a workout to see exercise-level set details.
  • Coach logic uses recent performance to suggest progression, deloads or exercise variations.

Designed for gym training

GymTracker is primarily a gym training app, but it also supports temporary home or outdoor workout adaptations when you cannot get to the gym.

  • Track regular gym sessions.
  • Use equipment-aware program generation.
  • Adapt a single workout for bodyweight, bands or dumbbells.
  • Keep your long-term program intact.

Privacy

GymTracker is designed to respect your privacy. You can log and review your training without creating an account.

Support & contact

If you encounter issues, have questions about how the app works or want to suggest features, you can contact the developer directly.

Is GymTracker free?

GymTracker is currently in closed testing. Core workout logging is designed to stay practical and accessible. A Pro model may be introduced later for advanced features.

Do I need an account or internet connection?

No account is required to use GymTracker. Workout logs and settings are stored locally on your device. Internet is mainly needed to download updates and access Google Play services.

Which devices are supported?

GymTracker is designed for Android phones and tablets running Android 8.0 or newer.
